SoccerNet is a comprehensive web platform designed to facilitate the connection between football agents and football players.It provides a seamless interface for players to showcase their skills, achievements, and career aspirations, while enabling agents to discover and connect with promising talent. The platform aims to bridge the gap between players and agents, fostering a professional network that enhances career opportunities and growth within the football industry and there is our feature below.
- Profile Management:
Comprehensive Profiles Players can create detailed profiles including personal information, career statistics, achievements, and a personal statement. Agents can showcase their portfolio, including players they represent and their achievements. Multimedia Uploads Players can upload images and CVs, providing a visual and comprehensive overview of their careers. Agents can upload promotional materials and successful client stories.
- Advanced Search and Filtering:
Player Search for Agents Agents can search for players based on various criteria such as position, age, nationality, and skill level. Agent Search for Players Players can find agents based on their specialization, reputation, and the types of players they represent.
- Direct Messaging and Communication:
Instant Messaging Secure messaging system allowing direct communication between players and agents. This feature facilitates initial contact and ongoing negotiations. Notifications: Users receive real-time notifications for new messages, profile views, and updates, ensuring they stay informed and engaged.
