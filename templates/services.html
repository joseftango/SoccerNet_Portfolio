- Player Profile Management:
This service allows players to create and manage their personal profiles. Players can upload their photos, CVs, and other relevant documents. They can update their personal information such as name, age, nationality, and playing position. The profile management service ensures that the players' profiles are always up-to-date and accurately reflect their skills and achievements.
- Agent Profile Management:
Agents can create and manage their profiles, highlighting their expertise, experience, and the players they have managed. This service enables agents to showcase their professional background and attract new players. Agents can also update their contact information and other relevant details to maintain effective communication with players.
- Search and Discovery:
This service provides a robust search engine that allows players and agents to find each other based on specific criteria. Players can search for agents based on their location, expertise, and success rate. Similarly, agents can search for players based on their skills, positions, and career achievements. This service ensures that users can quickly find the best matches for their needs.
- Messaging and Communication:
Secure and seamless communication is facilitated through an integrated messaging system. Players and agents can send and receive messages within the platform, ensuring all communications are centralized and easily accessible. This service supports real-time chat, allowing for instant interaction and decision-making.
- Contract Management:
This service provides tools for creating, reviewing, and managing contracts between players and agents. Contracts can be digitally signed within the platform, ensuring a secure and legally binding agreement. This service also offers contract templates and helps users track contract statuses and deadlines.
